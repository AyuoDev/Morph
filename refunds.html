Refund Policy

Last Updated: February 15, 2026

We want you to be satisfied with Morphara. This refund policy explains when and how you can request a refund for credit purchases and subscriptions.

1. Credit Purchases (One-Time)

✓ ELIGIBLE FOR REFUND:
  • Purchased within the last 7 days
  • Credits have NOT been used (no exports made)
  • Account in good standing (no violations)
✗ NOT ELIGIBLE FOR REFUND:
  • Credits purchased more than 7 days ago
  • Any credits have been used (even 1 export)
  • Credits were part of a promotional offer
  • Account has been banned or suspended

Refund Process:

  1. Email ayuodev@gmail.com within 7 days of purchase
  2. Include your account email and transaction ID (from Stripe receipt)
  3. We'll verify no credits were used
  4. If approved, refund processed within 5-10 business days
  5. Credits will be removed from your account upon refund

2. Subscription Plans (Premium & Studio)

✓ ELIGIBLE FOR REFUND:
  • New Subscriptions: Within 7 days of first payment
  • Billing Errors: Charged incorrectly or double-billed
  • Technical Issues: Service was unavailable for extended periods
  • Monthly credits have NOT been used
✗ NOT ELIGIBLE FOR REFUND:
  • Renewal charges (only first month may be refunded)
  • Partial month refunds (we don't prorate)
  • If monthly credits have been used
  • Subscription cancelled after 7-day window
  • Voluntary downgrade from Studio to Premium

Subscription Cancellation:

  • Cancel anytime from your account settings
  • Access continues until end of current billing period
  • No refund for remaining days in the current period
  • Unused monthly credits expire when subscription ends

3. Refund Timeframes

Payment Method Refund Time
Credit/Debit Card 5-10 business days
PayPal 3-5 business days
Other Methods Up to 14 business days

4. Special Circumstances

Account Errors

If we made an error (wrong charge amount, duplicate billing), contact us immediately. We'll issue a full refund regardless of time limits.

Service Outages

If Morphara is unavailable for more than 48 consecutive hours due to our fault, you may request a prorated refund for subscription time lost.

Fraudulent Charges

If you didn't authorize a charge, contact us immediately at ayuodev@gmail.com and your bank/card issuer. We'll cooperate with investigations.

5. Chargeback Policy

⚠️ Important: Filing a chargeback without contacting us first may result in account suspension.

  • Always contact us first before initiating a chargeback
  • We resolve 95%+ of issues within 48 hours
  • Chargebacks cost us fees and may result in permanent account closure
  • If you file a chargeback, we reserve the right to terminate your account

6. Non-Refundable Items

The following are NEVER refundable:

  • Promotional credits (free bonus credits)
  • Credits gifted by other users
  • Exported characters (once downloaded)
  • Custom work or support services
  • Third-party fees (bank charges, currency conversion)

7. How to Request a Refund

  1. Email Us: ayuodev@gmail.com
  2. Subject Line: "Refund Request - [Your Account Email]"
  3. Include:
    • Account email address
    • Transaction ID (from Stripe receipt)
    • Reason for refund request
    • Date of purchase
  4. Response Time: We'll reply within 48 hours (usually faster)
  5. Processing: Approved refunds processed within 5-10 business days

8. Refund Abuse

We reserve the right to deny refunds or terminate accounts for:

  • Repeated refund requests (pattern of abuse)
  • Using credits before requesting refund
  • Providing false information
  • Attempting to exploit the refund policy

9. Questions About This Policy

If you have questions about our refund policy, contact us:

  • Email: ayuodev@gmail.com
  • Discord: discord.gg/morphara
  • Response Time: 24-48 hours

Fair Refund Philosophy: We built Morphara to empower creators. If you're unhappy with your purchase and haven't used the credits, we'll refund you. Simple as that. Just reach out within 7 days.
